# Privacy Policy for Did You Know

**Effective date:** April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Did You Know application (the "App") handles information.

The App does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.  
All functionality operates entirely on your device.

---

## Summary

- The App does **not collect personal data**.
- The App does **not transmit any information** to the developer or third parties.
- The App does **not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services**.

---

## Local Data Storage

The App does not store any personal data.  
It may store minimal non-personal settings (such as display preferences) directly on your device.

This data:
- Remains on your device  
- Is not shared with anyone  
- Can be removed by uninstalling the App  

---

## Data Sharing

The developer does **not** sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.  
No data is collected, therefore no data can be shared.

---

Since the App does not collect or process personal data, most GDPR obligations do not apply.
